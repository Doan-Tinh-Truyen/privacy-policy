We DO NOT COLLECT, STORE, or HAVE ACCESS to or INTERFERE with any personal content you enter into the app.

This includes but is not limited to:

Task lists, plans, schedules.

All of the above data is stored locally and solely on your device, protected by encryption. We have no mechanism to collect or transfer this data to any server.

5. App Permissions and Specific Purposes
Storage Access Permission (Photos/Media):
Specific Purpose: Allows you to select images from your device library to attach or customize your interface.
Access Scope: The app only accesses specific media files that you explicitly and actively allow via the system file picker.
Notification Permission:
Specific Purpose: To display reminders and notifications for events, tasks, or important notes you have set.
Write to Storage Permission:
Specific Purpose: To create backup files, allowing you to protect your data. The app only accesses backup files during creation and restoration.

We COMMIT to NEVER selling, renting, or sharing any personal content you create in the app with any third party.
7. Data Security & Specific Risks
7.1. Security Measures

We use industry-standard encryption libraries in the app to protect data stored locally on your device.

7.2. Important Warning About PIN Protection Feature
The app provides a PIN protection feature as an optional additional security layer.
Important: The PIN is set and managed by you. Due to local encryption design, we have absolutely no ability to recover or reset your PIN if you forget it.

If you forget your PIN, you will not be able to access the protected data in the app. The only way to continue using the app is to completely delete app data, which will result in permanent loss of all unbacked data.

7.3. User Responsibility and Limitation of Liability
Since your personal data is stored entirely on your device, the ultimate responsibility for protection lies with you. You are advised to:
Protect your physical device with security methods.
Regularly and proactively back up important data using the app's backup feature.
Carefully remember your PIN or password.
Stay vigilant against malicious apps and keep your device OS updated.

We have taken commercially reasonable steps to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data stored locally on your device.

We are not responsible for any loss, disclosure, unauthorized access, damage, or destruction of data arising from events beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to: forgotten PIN, device theft or loss, hardware failure, user error, or malicious software actions.
